The number that moves a stock on earnings day is rarely the headline EPS - it's the surprise, how far the result lands from what analysts modelled, plus the forward guidance management gives. A company can beat and still fall if the beat was already priced in, or miss and rally if guidance is strong. Use the estimates here as the bar the market has set, watch the before-open versus after-close timing so you know when a name will move, and treat the beat/miss tags as a starting point, not the whole story.
