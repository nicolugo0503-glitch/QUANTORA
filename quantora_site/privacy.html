Privacy & your choices

Quantora is a free, no-login markets terminal. We are built to collect as little about you as possible, and to give you clear control over anything optional.

Last updated: July 2026

The short version

  • No account, no sign-up. We never ask for your name, email, passwords, or brokerage or bank credentials.
  • We use only essential cookies to run the site. Any analytics cookies are optional and off until you accept them.
  • We do not sell your personal information, and we honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) browser signal automatically.
  • Market data comes from third parties, may be delayed, and is for information only, not investment advice.

What we collect

Because Quantora requires no login, we do not collect personal identifiers like your name or email. Like nearly all websites, our hosting provider processes standard technical information (such as IP address and browser type) in server logs to operate the service, keep it secure, and prevent abuse. If you accept optional cookies, we may collect privacy-preserving, aggregated usage analytics (such as which pages are viewed) to improve the product. We do not build advertising profiles about you.

Cookies and similar technologies

Essential cookies are required for the site to function and are always on. Optional analytics cookies help us understand usage and improve Quantora; they are set only if you choose "Accept all" in our consent banner. If you "Decline optional," we do not set or share optional cookies. You can change your choice at any time using the control below.

Global Privacy Control (GPC)

If your browser or a privacy extension sends the Global Privacy Control signal, Quantora automatically treats it as an opt-out of any sale or sharing of personal information and does not set optional analytics cookies. You do not need to do anything else.

Do Not Sell or Share (California / CCPA-CPRA)

We do not sell your personal information for money. To opt out of any "sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, use the control above or enable GPC in your browser. California residents also have the right to know, delete, correct, and to be free from discrimination for exercising these rights.

Your rights (GDPR / EEA / UK)

Where GDPR or UK GDPR applies, you have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and to data portability, subject to legal limits. Because we hold very little that identifies you, some requests may be limited by the data actually available. Our lawful bases are legitimate interest (operating and securing the site) and consent (optional analytics).
