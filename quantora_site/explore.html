Screens & strategies

A starting library of quant screens and strategies - each one runs in a single click, and you can share or fork any of them with a link. This is the seed of a community library; today it is curated, and it will open up to your own saved screens.

Have a screen you run every week? Soon you will be able to save, publish, and fork screens under your own handle and build a track record. For now, every card here is shareable - hit Share to copy a link.
Screens run through Quantora's AI screener over US-listed stocks and return a starting watchlist with reasoning; they are AI-generated starting points, not live fundamental screens, and can be wrong or out of date. Strategy cards open the relevant Quantora engine. Educational only - not investment advice.
