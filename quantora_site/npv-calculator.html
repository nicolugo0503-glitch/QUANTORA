Compute the net present value (NPV) of a series of cash flows at your discount rate. Positive NPV means the investment adds value.

Calculator
How it works
Net present value discounts every future cash flow back to today and sums them. If the total is positive, the investment is worth more than it costs at your required rate of return; if negative, it destroys value.
NPV = sum CFt / (1 + rate)^t
