This is a squarified treemap - the layout Bloomberg and Finviz use because it packs tiles to be as square as possible, keeping areas honestly comparable and labels readable. Quantora computes it in your browser with a verified algorithm, sizing each tile by the magnitude of the day's move and coloring it on a perceptual green-to-red scale that stays readable and colorblind-considerate. Green clusters mean risk-on; a wall of red is a down day at a glance. Click any tile to open that ticker.
Movers via Financial Modeling Prep (may be delayed; the free feed has daily limits - if live data is unavailable a sample map is shown, labeled as such). Sizing is by absolute percentage move, not market cap. For information and education, not investment advice.
