52-week range & momentum

Every stock lives somewhere between its 52-week high and low - and where it sits, plus how fast it is moving, tells you a lot about its trend. Quantora pins any ticker inside its yearly range, breaks momentum down by horizon, and scores today's 3-month move against its own history. No login, five years of daily data, free.

Range position is (price − 52-week low) ÷ (52-week high − 52-week low), so 0% is at the low and 100% is at the high. Momentum figures are simple price changes over roughly 21, 63, 126 and 252 trading days and are price-only - they exclude dividends. The momentum percentile compares the current 3-month (63-day) return against every 3-month return across the available history. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
