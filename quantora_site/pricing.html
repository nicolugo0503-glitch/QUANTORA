Questions

Is there really a free tier?
Yes — the full terminal, all 60+ engines, live data and per-ticker research are free, no login required. Pro removes limits (real-time, unlimited watchlists/alerts, full history, exports).
What makes Quantora different?
Every number is computed by a unit-tested engine and shows its work — verified math, not a black box. Options Greeks, risk, macro, valuation, all checkable.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Subscriptions are managed through Stripe's secure customer portal — cancel or switch plans in a click.
Is this investment advice?
No. Quantora provides mathematical tools for analysis and education. It is not a registered investment adviser or broker-dealer.
