WACC Calculator

Calculate the weighted average cost of capital (WACC) - the blended after-tax rate a company pays to finance its assets, used as the discount rate in a DCF.

Calculator
How it works
WACC blends the cost of equity and the after-tax cost of debt, weighted by how much of each the company uses. Debt is cheaper because interest is tax-deductible. WACC is the standard hurdle rate for valuing a business.
WACC = (E/V).Re + (D/V).Rd.(1 - tax) , V = E + D
