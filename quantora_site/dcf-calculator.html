How it works
A DCF values a company as the present value of its future cash. Each projected free cash flow is discounted back at the discount rate (WACC); a terminal value captures everything beyond the forecast using a perpetual growth rate. Summing them gives enterprise value; subtract net debt for equity value.
EV = sum FCFt/(1+d)^t + [FCF_N.(1+g)/(d-g)]/(1+d)^N Equity value = EV - net debt
